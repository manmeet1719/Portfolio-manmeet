Everyone likes to order food online, from their favorite restaurants or as per their craving for particular food item. However, this case study main focuses on people who are away from their homes, working or studying in other cities or countries from their home.
They don’t get home cook food on daily basis either they eat or order from nearby restaurants or make food on their own. So this platform main focus is to fulfil the need of the users to get the home cooked food from the people who provide Tiffin Services.
My role for this project ranged from the research to design, that is the entire UI/UX work. All of the work was done on Figma.
